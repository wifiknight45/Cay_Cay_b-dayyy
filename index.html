Make a Bead Bracelet Like Caydence!

Caydence loves making bead bracelets (sometimes called candy jewelry). Here’s how you can make one too!

  1. Get Your Stuff: Grab stretchy cord (like elastic string), colorful beads (plastic or glass), and scissors. Ask a grown-up for help with cutting!
  2. Measure the Cord: Cut a piece of cord about 10 inches long. It should fit around your wrist with a little extra.
  3. Add Beads: String beads onto the cord. Mix colors and shapes—like pink and purple for Chappell Roan vibes! Leave about 2 inches empty at each end.
  4. Tie It: Tie a tight double knot with the two ends of the cord. Ask a grown-up to make sure it’s secure.
  5. Try It On: Slip it on your wrist. If it’s too loose or tight, adjust the knot or add/remove beads.
  6. Show It Off: Wear your bracelet and tell Caydence about it in a birthday message!

Tip: Use letter beads to spell “Caydence” or “11” for extra fun!
